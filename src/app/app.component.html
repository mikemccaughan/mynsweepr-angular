Play Mynsweepr

Key

Action

Space Enter

Reveals the cell. If the cell contains a mine, loses the game.

f F + (on the NumPad)

Flags a cell as containing a mine

r R - (either one)

Reveals surrounding cells, based on the number of mines nearby (shown as a number in the cell)

w W ↑ (up arrow) ↑ (up arrow 1) 8 (2)

Moves to the cell above the currently active cell. Does nothing if on the top row.

a A ← (left arrow) ← (left arrow 1) 4 (2)

Moves to the cell to the left the currently active cell. Does nothing if on the left-most column.

d D → (right arrow) → (right arrow 1) 6 (2)

Moves to the cell to the right the currently active cell. Does nothing if on the right-most column.

s S ↓ (down arrow) ↓ (down arrow 1) 2 (2)

Moves to the cell below the currently active cell. Does nothing if on the bottom-most row.

1 NumPad with NumLock off 2 NumPad with NumLock on
